If you click on a link to a third party website, such third party may also transmit cookies to you. This Privacy Policy does not cover the use of cookies by any third parties.

When we do this, we may receive a confirmation when you open an email from us. This confirmation helps us make emails more interesting and improve our service.

8. Is Personal Information About Me Secure?

We employ appropriate administrative, organizational, technical, and physical measures to protect your Personal Information, which we regularly review and update as necessary.

Fable practices encryption of data in rest (AES-256) and transit (HTTPS/TLS) for it's resources. Moreover, Personal data is accessible only by the security team.

Fable supports logging in to the system via social login provider (Google / Microsoft), in that case, your Fable account protection is an extension of Google / Microsoft's email account protection. Fable also allows users to log in with an email id and password, in that case, your account will be protected by email verification for your privacy and security.

You must prevent unauthorized access to your account and Personal Information by limiting access to your computer or device and browser by signing off after you have finished accessing your account.

We endeavor to protect the privacy of your account and other Personal Information we hold in our records, but we cannot guarantee complete security. Unauthorized entry or use, hardware or software failure, and other factors may compromise the security of user information at any time. The Websites may contain links to other sites. We are not responsible for the privacy policies and/or practices on other sites. When following a link to another site you should read that site’s privacy policy.
